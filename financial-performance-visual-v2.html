Process Flow Insights:

  • Streamlined 13-Step Process: Consolidated from 18 to 13 steps by combining natural workflows (close + reporting, KPIs + dashboards, margin + variance, pricing strategy + power, cash forecast + runway)
  • Mike's Central Financial Role: Ultimate accountability for all financial management, analysis, and decision-making with high owner dependency (4.58/5.0 - target: 3.5)
  • Kate's Supporting Function: Transaction coding, light bookkeeping, KPI tracking support, dashboard updates, administrative assistance
  • Outsourced Expertise: External bookkeepers handle technical month-end work and reconciliations, tax accountant provides strategic consultation
  • Jeremiah's Operational Input: Consulted on operational efficiency, margin performance, revenue growth strategy, and pricing decisions
  • Three-Color System: Blue (Tracking/Measurement), Orange (Analysis/Forecasting), Green (Guidance/Recommendations)
  • Integration Challenge: QuickBooks → Janmar one-way sync with manual Google Sheets analysis creates 30+ day close cycle (target: 10 days)
  • Strategic Priority: Reduce owner dependency through automation, accelerate close process, implement real-time dashboards, systematize unit economics
  • Growth Context: Building infrastructure to support 24% revenue growth ($3.4M to $4.2M) and Spring 2026 expansion (8 new hires)
  • Margin Focus: Closing 4-point gap (41% to 45% gross margin) through pricing optimization (primary lever: 3% increase = $102K profit) and enhancement ratio improvement (17.5% to 40%)
  • Cadence Alignment: Bi-weekly labor tracking aligns with payroll, monthly reviews for most metrics, quarterly strategic analysis, annual planning cycle
